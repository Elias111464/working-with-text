CHEESE AND ZUCCHINI CROQUETTES

Time: 20 minutes

Are you passionate about croquettes? Then go ahead and try these cheese and zucchini ones: they are made in less than 20 minutes and are delicious. In addition, they are very easy to prepare, even for beginners in the world of cooking.

Ingredients (for 4 people)

1 zucchini

150 grams of Gruyere cheese

1 garlic

40 grams of flour

40 grams of butter

200 ml of milk

100 grams of breadcrumbs

1 egg

Basil

Pepper

Oil and salt
